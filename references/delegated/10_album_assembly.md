# Delegated: Album Assembly After Audio

## Task
Finalize the album after generated audio candidates have been reviewed.

## Read
- all generation packets
- all post_generation_eval.json files
- foundation/*.md
- planning/*.md
- selected render paths

## Output files
- reviews/sequence_after_audio.md
- release_package/final_tracklist.md
- release_package/final_lyrics.md
- release_package/metadata.json
- release_package/cover_direction.md
- release_package/liner_notes.md
- release_package/credits.md

## Checks
- First three tracks still make the promise after hearing audio.
- Lead single candidate is actually one of the strongest generated tracks.
- Weakest track is revised, cut, or justified.
- Adjacent tracks have contrast in energy, groove, vocal intensity, or texture.
- Motifs recur intentionally, not accidentally.
- Closer lands emotionally and sonically.
- Album feels sequenced, not listed.

## Output summary
Include:
- final sequence.
- why each track is in position.
- any changed ordering from the paper plan.
- selected candidate per track.
- remaining risks.
- final release readiness score.
