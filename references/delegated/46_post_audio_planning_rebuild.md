# Delegated 46 — Post-Audio Planning Rebuild

## Purpose
After selected renders exist, rebuild the album's planning documents from the reality of the audio. The paper plan is only a hypothesis. Actual renders determine the final album.

## Read
- `references/schemas/post_audio_rebuild.schema.json`
- selected candidates and post-generation reviews
- timestamped listening notes
- final audio QC if available
- `planning/tracklist.md`
- `planning/album_arc.md`
- `planning/album_energy_map.md`
- `planning/transition_map.md`
- `foundation/album_canon.json`

## Write

```text
planning/post_audio_tracklist.md
planning/post_audio_album_arc.md
planning/post_audio_energy_map.md
planning/post_audio_transition_map.md
planning/post_audio_rebuild.json
reviews/sequence_after_audio.md
```

## Tasks

1. Re-describe every selected track based on actual audio.
2. Identify where the render changed the intended role.
3. Recompute opener promise, early range, middle pacing, climax, and closer aftertaste.
4. Identify redundant adjacent tracks.
5. Identify accidental tonal or energy gaps.
6. Recommend reordering, cutting, regenerating, or adding connective material.
7. Update album canon with accepted render facts.
8. Create propagation debts for stale release or planning artifacts.

## Pass/fail

A release-package album cannot pass if the final sequence is based only on pre-render planning when selected audio exists.
