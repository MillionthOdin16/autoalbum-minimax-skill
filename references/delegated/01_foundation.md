# Delegated: Foundation

## Task
Create the album foundation before track drafting.

## Read

- project_config.json

- references/craft/professional_album_craft.md

## Output files
- foundation/album_thesis.md
- foundation/sound_signature.md
- foundation/lyric_voice.md
- foundation/vocal_identity.md
- foundation/reference_translation.md
- foundation/motif_ledger.md
- foundation/vocabulary_ledger.md
- foundation/generation_constraints.md

## Requirements

### album_thesis.md
Include:
- 1-sentence album thesis.
- 3-sentence narrative/emotional test: setup, conflict, what remains.
- why this must be an album, not one song.
- what the listener should carry out.
- what would make it generic and must be avoided.

### sound_signature.md
Include:
- genre/subgenre family.
- sonic DNA: 4-7 recurring traits.
- allowed contrast zones.
- tempo range and energy range.
- harmonic color palette.
- drum/bass philosophy.
- vocal production philosophy.
- texture/reverb/stereo world.
- hard avoids.

### lyric_voice.md
Include:
- point of view.
- diction.
- image bank.
- sentence/line tendencies.
- taboo words/phrases.
- allowed motifs.
- how chorus language differs from verse language.

### vocal_identity.md
Include:
- lead vocal character.
- register and delivery.
- emotional temperature.
- harmony/ad-lib rules.
- how the vocal changes across the album.

### reference_translation.md
If references are used, translate them into attributes:
- rhythm/groove.
- vocal behavior.
- production texture.
- energy curve.
- mix depth.
Preserve exact user-supplied references and convert them into audible, promptable controls: rhythm/groove, drums, bass, harmony, vocal behavior, hook mechanics, arrangement, and mix texture.

### motif_ledger.md
Create initial motifs with:
- motif name.
- lyrical form.
- sonic form.
- first planned appearance.
- later planned transformation.
- reason for recurrence.

### vocabulary_ledger.md
Separate:
- intentional recurring phrases.
- words reserved for specific tracks.
- banned generic phrases.

### generation_constraints.md
Convert foundation into MiniMax controls:
- prompt constants used across tracks.
- variables that change per track.
- hard constraints.
- soft guidance.
- model risk notes.
