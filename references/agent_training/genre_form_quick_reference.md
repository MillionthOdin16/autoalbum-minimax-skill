# Genre and Form Quick Reference

This is a practical quick reference for choosing forms, grooves, and controls. It is not a substitute for the style catalog.

## Rap / trap

Common traits:
- 70-90 BPM feel or 140-180 double-time grid
- 808 bass and programmed drums
- hook can be chant, sung, ad-lib, phrase, or beat-drop identity
- flow/cadence is central

Common forms:
```text
[Intro] [Hook] [Verse] [Hook] [Verse] [Hook] [Outro]
[Intro] [Verse] [Chorus] [Verse] [Chorus] [Bridge/Break] [Chorus]
```

Control prompt with:
- drum bounce
- 808 behavior
- vocal delivery
- ad-lib behavior
- hook type
- mix darkness/brightness

## Pop

Common traits:
- immediate title/hook
- clear emotional premise
- strong contrast between verse and chorus
- polished vocal production

Common form:
```text
[Intro] [Verse] [Pre Chorus] [Chorus] [Verse] [Pre Chorus] [Chorus] [Bridge] [Chorus] [Outro]
```

Control prompt with:
- vocal clarity
- chorus lift
- drum/bass polish
- synth/guitar/piano palette
- post-chorus if earworm needed

## R&B / alt-R&B

Common traits:
- groove pocket
- intimate vocals
- extended harmony or moody loops
- negative space
- vocal stacks/ad-libs

Control prompt with:
- vocal intimacy
- harmony color
- percussion pocket
- bass warmth
- reverb/dryness
- sensual/psychological lyric posture

## Electronic / dance

Common traits:
- energy cycles
- builds, drops/hooks, breaks
- bass and drum design
- repeated motifs

Common form:
```text
[Intro] [Build Up] [Hook/Inst] [Break] [Build Up] [Hook/Inst] [Outro]
```

Control prompt with:
- BPM feel
- kick pattern
- bass type
- build/drop behavior
- synth texture
- club vs headphone context

## Reggaeton / Latin urbano

Common traits:
- dembow/tresillo rhythmic gravity
- danceable percussion
- hook-forward phrasing
- Spanish/Spanglish possible depending user request

Control prompt with:
- dembow rhythm
- percussion texture
- vocal tone
- bass movement
- hook chant
- party/romance/street posture

## Country / folk

Common traits:
- story and place
- acoustic guitar/pedal steel/fiddle/banjo depending lane
- title as image, punchline, or emotional turn
- clear diction

Control prompt with:
- vocal sincerity/twang level
- instrumentation
- story perspective
- chorus lift
- bridge revelation

## Rock / pop-punk / alt

Common traits:
- guitar and live drums central
- chorus impact
- physical energy
- bridge/breakdown contrast

Control prompt with:
- guitar tone
- drum energy
- vocal intensity
- chorus gang/double vocals
- dynamic shift

## MiniMax caution

Do not assume MiniMax will obey exact keys, exact BPM, exact bar counts, or precise engineering values. Use them as guidance, not final truth. Judge actual renders.
