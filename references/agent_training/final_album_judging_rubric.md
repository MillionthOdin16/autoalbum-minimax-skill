# Final Album Judging Rubric

Use this to judge whether a project is professionally credible. The goal is not to prove the pipeline ran; the goal is to decide whether the album deserves to exist.

## Album-level scores

### 1. Concept and identity
9-10: distinct thesis, audible world, clear identity.
7-8: coherent but less surprising.
5-6: understandable but generic.
0-4: mood-board only.

### 2. Song strength
9-10: multiple tracks have real replay value and memorable moments.
7-8: solid songs with some standout hooks.
5-6: competent but few moments stick.
0-4: filler or weak hooks dominate.

### 3. Style fidelity
9-10: requested style/artist/song lane is specific and preserved without adjacent-style confusion.
7-8: mostly in lane with minor drift.
5-6: broad genre is present but fingerprint weak.
0-4: generic or wrong style.

### 4. Album cohesion
9-10: shared DNA is audible and meaningful.
7-8: cohesive but some generic links.
5-6: connected by theme more than sound.
0-4: playlist, not album.

### 5. Album contrast
9-10: each track has a distinct role and energy/texture/lyric function.
7-8: most tracks distinct.
5-6: several redundant tracks.
0-4: same song repeatedly.

### 6. Sequencing
9-10: opener promises, early run convinces, middle develops, closer lands.
7-8: good sequence with minor sag.
5-6: acceptable but not compelling.
0-4: arbitrary order.

### 7. Lyric/topline quality
9-10: lyrics are specific, performable, style-matched, and hook-centered.
7-8: strong with minor issues.
5-6: readable but not consistently songlike.
0-4: generic/awkward.

### 8. Production/render quality
9-10: selected renders sound intentional, style-correct, and album-ready.
7-8: good candidates with minor issues.
5-6: usable demos, not release caliber.
0-4: artifacts, generic production, or weak vocal output.

## Major-release readiness test

The project cannot be called release-grade unless:

- at least 30-40% of tracks feel like standouts or essential album moments
- no track is obvious filler
- opener is compelling within the first 30 seconds
- lead single candidate has a memorable hook
- closer changes the album's aftertaste
- style fingerprints are audible in generated results
- final audio QC has no unresolved blockers
- every selected render has provenance

## Cut/revise decisions

- **Cut** if a track has no unique album function and weak hook.
- **Revise lyrics** if audio follows prompt but song still lacks impact.
- **Revise prompt** if lyrics are good but render misses style or arrangement.
- **Regenerate same packet** if packet is strong but render has artifacts or weak take.
- **Resequence** if track is good but location weakens album arc.

## Final judgment statements

Use one:

```text
RELEASE_GRADE: ready, no major blockers.
STRONG_DEMO_PACKAGE: musically strong but needs final audio/mastering/workflow outside skill.
PROMPT_READY_ONLY: packets are ready, audio not yet generated.
REVISION_REQUIRED: specific blockers must be fixed.
NOT_COMPETITIVE: project fails major song/style/album standards.
```
