# Detailed Electronic and Dance Fingerprints

## Deep house
- **Tempo / feel:** 118-124 BPM, four-on-the-floor, relaxed but moving.
- **Drums:** warm kick, soft clap/snare, shakers, subtle swing, restrained fills.
- **Bass:** round, pulsing, syncopated, often sidechained gently.
- **Harmony:** minor 7ths, lush pads, jazzy stabs, moody but smooth.
- **Vocals:** short phrases, breathy topline, chopped vocal hooks, not lyric-heavy.
- **Hook mechanics:** groove + vocal phrase + bass movement, not a rock/pop chorus.
- **Arrangement:** gradual filter changes, breakdown, re-entry, no aggressive EDM drop unless fusion.
- **Mix:** warm, spacious, club low end, smooth highs.
- **Failure modes:** generic festival house, weak bass, too many lyrics, overbright synths.

## Tech house
- **Tempo / feel:** 124-128 BPM; crisp, repetitive, club-functional.
- **Drums:** tight kick, sharp clap, busy hats, percussion loops, snare rolls sparingly.
- **Bass:** short, hooky bass phrase is often the identity.
- **Harmony:** minimal; rhythmic and textural rather than chord-heavy.
- **Vocals:** short commands, chopped phrases, attitude lines, callouts.
- **Hook mechanics:** bass riff + vocal snippet + drum groove.
- **Arrangement:** DJ-friendly intro/outro, breakdown, build, drop/re-entry.
- **Mix:** punchy kick/bass separation, mono-compatible low end.
- **Failure modes:** too song-like, weak bass hook, cluttered chord pads.

## Melodic techno
- **Tempo / feel:** 120-126 BPM, hypnotic, long-form tension.
- **Drums:** steady kick, restrained percussion, rolling momentum.
- **Bass:** deep pulse, often less funky than house.
- **Harmony:** arpeggiated minor motifs, modal progressions, emotional synth leads.
- **Vocals:** minimal or atmospheric; vocal should not overexplain.
- **Hook mechanics:** synth motif and tension arc.
- **Arrangement:** long builds, evolving layers, breakdown/rebuild, dramatic re-entry.
- **Mix:** wide synths, deep kick, cinematic reverb.
- **Failure modes:** trance cliché, no development, too many pop vocals.

## Drum and bass
- **Tempo / feel:** 160-180 BPM; fast breakbeat energy, half-time vocal possible.
- **Drums:** breakbeats, ghost notes, shuffling hats, snare weight.
- **Bass:** sub-driven, Reese bass or liquid bass depending substyle.
- **Harmony:** liquid DnB uses lush chords; darker DnB uses minimal dissonance.
- **Vocals:** short melodic toplines or MC-style phrases; leave space for drums.
- **Hook mechanics:** bassline, breakbeat identity, vocal refrain.
- **Arrangement:** intro/build/drop/second drop; dynamic filter and drum variation.
- **Mix:** fast transient clarity, sub discipline.
- **Failure modes:** generic fast pop song, muddy breaks, weak sub.

## Synthwave / retro electro pop
- **Tempo / feel:** 80-115 BPM or driving 120 BPM; cinematic retro pulse.
- **Drums:** gated snares, electronic toms, steady kick, nostalgic fills.
- **Bass:** pulsing analog-style synth bass, octave movement.
- **Harmony:** minor keys, suspended chords, heroic/nostalgic cadences.
- **Vocals:** airy, dramatic, reverb-rich or instrumental lead-synth focus.
- **Hook mechanics:** lead synth motif + chorus/title phrase.
- **Arrangement:** slow-burn intro, big chorus pads, guitar/synth solo optional.
- **Mix:** glossy, wide, reverb, neon atmosphere.
- **Failure modes:** cheesy 80s parody, no modern low-end control, overlong intro.
