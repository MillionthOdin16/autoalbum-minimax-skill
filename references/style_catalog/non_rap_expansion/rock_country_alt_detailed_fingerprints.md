# Detailed Rock, Country, and Alternative Fingerprints

## Pop-punk / emo pop
- **Tempo / feel:** 150-190 BPM or half-time chorus; urgent, youthful, cathartic.
- **Drums:** driving kick/snare, crash choruses, tom fills into hooks.
- **Bass:** eighth-note drive, supports guitars and kick.
- **Harmony:** power chords, I-V-vi-IV variants, modal mixture for emotional lift.
- **Vocals:** nasal/strained urgency, gang vocals, shoutable hooks.
- **Hook mechanics:** title phrase, high-energy chorus, emotional specificity.
- **Arrangement:** quick intro, verse drop, pre-chorus lift, explosive chorus, bridge breakdown.
- **Mix:** bright guitars, punchy drums, vocal upfront.
- **Failure modes:** generic rock with no youth/catharsis, chorus too wordy, guitars too polite.

## Modern indie rock
- **Tempo / feel:** 90-140 BPM; human groove, slightly imperfect performance feel.
- **Drums:** live-feeling kit, room tone, organic fills.
- **Bass:** melodic but restrained, supports guitar/vocal interplay.
- **Harmony:** guitar voicings, modal color, less obvious cadences.
- **Vocals:** conversational, characterful, not over-polished.
- **Hook mechanics:** melodic motif, lyrical angle, guitar riff, or chorus phrase.
- **Arrangement:** dynamics through texture, not only volume.
- **Mix:** guitars and room ambience matter; avoid sterile pop sheen.
- **Failure modes:** vague alternative mush, no riff/motif, vocals too polished.

## Shoegaze / dream pop
- **Tempo / feel:** 70-120 BPM; floating, immersive, textural.
- **Drums:** steady, sometimes buried; rhythm supports atmosphere.
- **Bass:** simple, grounding, often follows root motion.
- **Harmony:** lush chords, suspended color, droning or circular progressions.
- **Vocals:** soft, blended into texture, less lyric-forward.
- **Hook mechanics:** texture + melodic haze; chorus may bloom rather than punch.
- **Arrangement:** layers of guitars/synths, feedback swells, reverb clouds.
- **Mix:** wide, washed, controlled low end; vocals not too sharp.
- **Failure modes:** mud, no song center, reverb hiding weak writing.

## Traditional country storytelling
- **Tempo / feel:** 70-110 BPM; narrative clarity and emotional directness.
- **Drums:** simple backbeat or brushes; groove should not overpower lyric.
- **Bass:** root-fifth or walking feel; steady support.
- **Harmony:** I-IV-V, vi, secondary colors sparingly; cadences support story payoff.
- **Instruments:** acoustic guitar, pedal steel, fiddle, mandolin, piano, clean electric.
- **Vocals:** plainspoken, direct, believable; twang/character if style requires.
- **Hook mechanics:** title line carries story turn or emotional thesis.
- **Arrangement:** lyric-first; instrumental fills answer vocal lines.
- **Failure modes:** Nashville cliché pileup, generic truck/beer imagery, no story turn.

## Modern country-pop
- **Tempo / feel:** 80-125 BPM; polished, hook-forward, hybrid drums possible.
- **Drums:** country backbeat with pop polish; snaps/claps/electronic layers possible.
- **Bass:** clean, supportive, sometimes pop sub under acoustic texture.
- **Harmony:** accessible progressions, big chorus lift.
- **Vocals:** intimate verse, big chorus, clean doubles/harmonies.
- **Hook mechanics:** title phrase, everyday scene, emotional twist.
- **Arrangement:** acoustic identity plus radio-pop architecture.
- **Mix:** bright vocal, clean low end, guitars/pedal steel for identity.
- **Failure modes:** pop song with token banjo, cliché lyrics, chorus not big enough.

## Folk / acoustic singer-songwriter
- **Tempo / feel:** 60-105 BPM; intimate, lyric-forward.
- **Drums:** none, brushed, or minimal percussion.
- **Bass:** sparse, acoustic or soft electric.
- **Harmony:** simple chords with emotional voicings; capos/open tunings can be implied.
- **Vocals:** close, unvarnished, emotionally credible.
- **Hook mechanics:** lyric turn, repeated image, melodic refrain.
- **Arrangement:** space, dynamics, instrumental response to lyric.
- **Mix:** natural room, acoustic detail, vocal realism.
- **Failure modes:** poem over chords, no melodic center, too much production polish.
