# Artist Profiles: Pop, R&B, Global, Rock, and Electronic

## artist.weeknd_dark_rnb_synthpop_lane

**Reference anchors:** The Weeknd, dark R&B, Trilogy, After Hours, Dawn FM, synthwave pop.

**Core lane:** shadowy R&B with falsetto, nightlife melancholy, cinematic synths, rumbling bass, and pop hooks.

**Tempo:** 80–110 for dark R&B; 110–125 for synth-pop/dance lane.

**Drums:** sleek electronic drums, sparse dark R&B percussion or 80s pop drive.

**Bass:** rumbling sub or pulsing synth bass.

**Harmony:** minor, cinematic, dream-pop/shoegaze/synthwave colors, dramatic movement.

**Vocal:** smooth falsetto, haunted lead, layered harmonies, emotionally distant but intense.

**Lyric mode:** desire, regret, alienation, fame, nightlife, self-destruction.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A dark R&B/synth-pop track in a Weeknd lane, with haunted falsetto vocals, rumbling bass, sleek electronic drums, nocturnal synths, and a cinematic late-night atmosphere. The chorus should widen into a memorable pop moment while keeping the emotional world shadowy and seductive.
```

## artist.sza_alt_rnb_diaristic_lane

**Reference anchors:** SZA, Ctrl, SOS, alternative R&B, diaristic R&B.

**Core lane:** conversational, emotionally messy alt-R&B with pocket-drifting vocals, lush atmosphere, and diary-like lyric honesty.

**Tempo:** 65–100 BPM, sometimes brighter hybrids.

**Drums:** soft trap/R&B drums, understated percussion, groove over grid.

**Bass:** warm sub/electric bass cushion.

**Harmony:** extended chords, neo-soul/jazz color, dreamy pads.

**Vocal:** intimate, imperfect, talk-sing phrasing, spontaneous timing, layered background vocals, delayed/reversed/sliced textures.

**Lyric mode:** insecurity, desire, jealousy, healing, self-worth, nonlinear relationship details.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An alternative R&B song in a SZA lane, with lush atmospheric chords, warm low-end, soft syncopated drums, and intimate conversational vocals that drift around the pocket. The lyrics should feel diaristic, emotionally messy, and specific, with a vulnerable hook rather than a polished slogan.
```

## artist.billie_eilish_minimal_dark_pop_lane

**Reference anchors:** Billie Eilish, Finneas-style minimal pop, dark pop, whisper pop.

**Core lane:** minimalist dark pop with close whispered vocals, sub bass, strange small sounds, and dramatic dynamic shifts.

**Tempo:** 65–110 BPM.

**Drums:** sparse kick, snaps, foley, tiny percussion, occasional distorted impact.

**Bass:** deep sub, restrained but central.

**Harmony:** eerie minor/modal, simple but tense.

**Vocal:** whisper-close lead, intimate doubles, stacked harmonies at key moments.

**Lyric mode:** secrecy, body/emotion detail, danger, self-observation, dark humor, vulnerability.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A minimalist dark-pop track in a Billie Eilish lane, with close whispered vocals, deep restrained sub bass, sparse foley-like percussion, and an eerie minor atmosphere. Keep the verses intimate and almost ASMR-close, then let the chorus bloom through layered harmonies and controlled dynamic contrast.
```

## artist.frank_ocean_experimental_rnb_lane

**Reference anchors:** Frank Ocean, Blonde, Channel Orange, experimental R&B, art-pop soul.

**Core lane:** intimate experimental R&B with non-linear structure, sparse arrangements, diaristic storytelling, and emotional restraint.

**Tempo:** 60–100 BPM.

**Drums:** sparse or absent; soft electronic or organic pulses.

**Bass:** warm, minimal, sometimes absent for weightless sections.

**Harmony:** guitar/keys, extended chords, unexpected modulation, suspended color.

**Vocal:** tender, restrained, layered, pitch/formant shifts optional, background stacks.

**Lyric mode:** memory, desire, identity, spiritual ache, specificity without explanation.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An experimental R&B track in a Frank Ocean lane, with sparse guitar or keyboard harmony, warm intimate vocals, nonlinear arrangement, and subtle electronic textures. The song should feel diaristic and emotionally restrained, letting small details carry the weight rather than forcing a conventional pop climax.
```

## artist.ariana_grande_rnb_pop_vocal_lane

**Reference anchors:** Ariana Grande, R&B-pop, vocal runs, trap-pop, stacked harmonies.

**Core lane:** polished R&B-pop with agile lead vocals, stacked harmonies, trap/R&B drums, and glamorous melodic hooks.

**Tempo:** 70–115 BPM, or 120+ for dance-pop lane.

**Drums:** trap-pop drums, snaps, claps, soft 808s, polished percussion.

**Harmony:** R&B/jazz-pop chords, smooth progressions.

**Vocal:** agile, melismatic, high register, harmonies, whistle-like ad-libs if requested.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A polished R&B-pop track in an Ariana Grande lane, with smooth trap-pop drums, lush harmonies, agile lead vocals, and a glamorous hook. Let the chorus showcase stacked vocal layers and tasteful runs while keeping the groove light and modern.
```

## artist.taylor_swift_story_pop_lane

**Reference anchors:** Taylor Swift, narrative pop, country-pop, synth-pop storytelling, diaristic songwriter.

**Core lane:** highly legible narrative songwriting with title-centered hooks, emotional specificity, and clean pop/country/synth-pop structures.

**Tempo:** 75–125 BPM.

**Drums:** varies by era: acoustic country/pop kit, synth-pop drum machine, indie-folk soft percussion.

**Harmony:** clear progressions, strong pre-chorus lift, chorus payoff.

**Vocal:** conversational, intimate-to-anthemic, lyric clarity prioritized.

**Lyric mode:** scenes, relationship autopsy, memory objects, time/place, emotional twist.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A narrative pop song in a Taylor Swift lane, with clear storytelling verses, a tension-building pre-chorus, and a title-centered chorus that turns a specific emotional scene into a universal hook. Keep the vocal clear and intimate, with polished production that supports lyric detail.
```

## artist.bad_bunny_urbano_lane

**Reference anchors:** Bad Bunny, reggaeton, Latin trap, urbano, Puerto Rican global pop.

**Core lane:** reggaeton/Latin trap/pop flexibility with dembow groove, melodic rap, confident charisma, and modern urbano textures.

**Tempo:** 85–100 BPM for reggaeton; 120–160 for trap variants.

**Drums:** dembow or trap drums, crisp percussion, club-ready pulse.

**Bass:** clean 808/synth bass, strong but not muddy.

**Vocal:** Spanish melodic rap/singing, relaxed confidence, playful phrasing, chantable hooks.

**Lyric mode:** romance, swagger, loneliness, nightlife, beach/club energy, emotional bravado.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern urbano track in a Bad Bunny lane, blending reggaeton dembow with Latin trap attitude, clean 808 bass, crisp percussion, and relaxed Spanish melodic rap vocals. The hook should be charismatic, simple, and globally danceable.
```

## artist.burna_boy_afrofusion_lane

**Tempo:** 95–115 BPM.

**Drums:** syncopated percussion, shakers, rim clicks, danceable pocket.

**Bass:** warm melodic bassline.

**Harmony:** guitars, keys, bright but grounded chord movement.

**Vocal:** rich, relaxed, commanding, melodic, often call-response.

**Lyric mode:** pride, love, resilience, desire, social awareness, celebration.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An Afrofusion track in a Burna Boy lane, with warm syncopated percussion, melodic bass, clean guitar/keys, and a commanding relaxed vocal that blends Afrobeats, dancehall, and global pop. The hook should feel effortless, confident, and rhythmically infectious.
```

## artist.fred_again_emotional_house_lane

**Tempo:** 120–132 BPM.

**Drums:** house/UK garage influenced, soft but propulsive.

**Bass:** warm club low-end.

**Harmony:** piano/synth loops, melancholy but hopeful.

**Vocal:** chopped vocal phrases, human snippets, intimate repetition, sometimes spoken sample feel.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An emotional house track in a Fred again.. lane, with chopped intimate vocal fragments, warm club drums, piano/synth loops, and a build from private diary-like vulnerability into dancefloor release. Keep the production human, imperfect, and cathartic rather than glossy festival EDM.
```

## artist.tame_impala_psych_pop_lane

**Reference anchors:** Tame Impala, psychedelic pop, Currents lane, psych rock/electronic pop.

**Core lane:** psychedelic pop with phaser/chorus guitars, analog synths, hypnotic drums, dreamy vocals, and bass-forward grooves.

**Tempo:** 80–125 BPM.

**Drums:** live-feeling but processed, compressed, roomy/psychedelic.

**Bass:** melodic, prominent, warm.

**Harmony:** colorful psych-pop chords, modal shifts, dreamy progressions.

**Vocal:** airy, reverb/delay, soft but hooky.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A psychedelic pop track in a Tame Impala lane, with warm analog synths, phaser-washed guitars, melodic bass, processed live-feeling drums, and airy reverb vocals. The song should feel dreamy and hypnotic, with a chorus that floats but still hooks.
```

## artist.olivia_rodrigo_pop_punk_diary_lane

**Reference anchors:** Olivia Rodrigo, pop-punk heartbreak, diaristic alt-pop, teenage angst.

**Core lane:** diaristic pop/rock with explosive choruses, guitars, emotional specificity, and theatrical anger/sadness.

**Tempo:** 90–180 BPM depending on ballad vs punk track.

**Drums/guitars:** pop-rock live drums, distorted guitars for catharsis, piano/guitar for ballads.

**Vocal:** intimate verses, belted/shouted chorus, emotional cracks.

**Lyric mode:** jealousy, heartbreak, resentment, self-awareness, sharp one-liners.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A diaristic pop-punk/alt-pop track in an Olivia Rodrigo lane, with intimate confessional verses, explosive distorted-guitar choruses, live punchy drums, and a hook built from a sharp emotional one-liner. The vocal should move from wounded detail to cathartic anger.
```

**Harmony:** minor/nostalgic, orchestral, piano/guitar.

**Vocal:** languid, breathy, low-to-mid register, romantic fatalism.

**Lyric mode:** doomed romance, nostalgia, Americana myth, glamour, sadness, dangerous devotion.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A slow cinematic pop ballad in a Lana Del Rey lane, with languid breathy vocals, vintage piano/guitar, soft strings, and a melancholy Americana atmosphere. The lyrics should feel romantic, fatalistic, and image-rich, with the chorus drifting like a doomed memory.
```
