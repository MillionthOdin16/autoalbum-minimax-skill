# Artist Profiles: Hip-Hop and Rap

These profiles are practical generation fingerprints. Use them when a user names an artist, an artist era, or a track/album associated with that artist. For maximum MiniMax usefulness, keep the name as a reference anchor and also compile the concrete musical fingerprint into the prompt.

---

## artist.juicy_j_three_6_mafia_lane

**Reference anchors:** Juicy J, Three 6 Mafia, DJ Paul, Memphis crunk, horror-crunk, Memphis rap.

**Core lane:** dark Memphis crunk-trap; club command energy; hypnotic repetition; physical 808/cowbell bounce.

**Tempo:** 70–85 BPM half-time or 140–170 double-time perception.

**Drums:** TR-808-style kick/sub, sharp snares, claps, cowbell, syncopated hats, looped percussion.

**Bass:** thick, repetitive, sub-heavy, more physical than melodic.

**Harmony/texture:** eerie minor synths, horror score color, sparse bells/piano, lo-fi or gritty edge.

**Vocal delivery:** shouted, commanding, chant-like, short repeated phrases, ad-libs in every gap, crowd-response posture.

**Lyric mode:** club commands, intoxicated nightlife, hustle/flex, menace, dark humor, simple memorable slogans.

**Hook mechanics:** one-line or two-line chant repeated until hypnotic; group vocal feel desirable.

**Arrangement:** short intro, beat establishes quickly, hook arrives early, verses ride the loop, breaks/dropouts for ad-libs.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A raw Memphis crunk-trap anthem in the lane of Juicy J and Three 6 Mafia, built around a slow half-time bounce with thick 808 sub, sharp snares, syncopated hi-hats, and a persistent cowbell pulse over an eerie minor synth loop. The vocal should be commanding, shouted, chant-like, and full of short ad-libs, with a repetitive club hook made for a crowd.
```

**Track functions:** club single, villain-energy cut, opener with immediate identity, mid-album spike.

**Avoid/failure modes:** too melodic, too polished, no cowbell/bounce, long intros, complex lyrics that fight the chant.

## artist.kendrick_lamar_cinematic_concept_lane

**Reference anchors:** Kendrick Lamar, good kid m.A.A.d city, To Pimp a Butterfly, conscious West Coast rap, jazz rap, narrative rap.

**Core lane:** concept-driven rap with cinematic sequencing, perspective shifts, moral conflict, and musical ambition.

### Era/lane options

#### `kendrick_gkmc_cinematic_west_coast`
- **Sound:** low-key/downbeat West Coast hip-hop, atmospheric beats, tight bass, subtle hooks.
- **Narrative:** coming-of-age, skits/scene logic, neighborhood pressure, family/community detail.
- **Vocal:** multiple voices, storytelling rap, conversational-to-intense dynamics.
- **Prompt fragment:**

```text
A cinematic West Coast concept-rap track in a Kendrick Lamar good kid m.A.A.d city lane, with low-key atmospheric production, tight bass, subtle hooks, and detailed narrative verses that feel like scenes from a short film. Use perspective shifts and moral tension rather than generic conscious-rap slogans.
```

#### `kendrick_tpab_jazz_funk_spoken_word`
- **Sound:** jazz, funk, soul, live bass/drums/horns, spoken-word intensity.
- **Narrative:** political, personal, community-centered, spiritually conflicted.
- **Vocal:** elastic rap, spoken word, performance shifts, emotional escalation.
- **Prompt fragment:**

```text
A jazz-funk conscious rap track in a Kendrick Lamar To Pimp a Butterfly lane, blending live-feeling drums, electric bass, horns, soul harmony, and spoken-word intensity. The vocal should move between precise rap, character performance, and poetic urgency, with the beat reacting like a band rather than looping flatly.
```

#### `kendrick_minimal_anthem_damn_lane`
- **Sound:** starker, more minimal, high-contrast rap/pop structures, sharp drums, memorable hooks.
- **Vocal:** direct, controlled, intense, hook-aware.
- **Prompt fragment:**

```text
A stark high-contrast rap anthem in a Kendrick Lamar DAMN-era lane, with minimal but hard-hitting production, sharp drums, negative space, and a hook that turns a moral/emotional contradiction into a concise chant.
```

**Lyric mode:** specific scenes, internal contradiction, cultural/political pressure, self-interrogation, character voices.

**Hook mechanics:** can be chant, repeated phrase, refrain, or conceptual phrase; hook should sharpen the thesis.

**Album use:** concept album center, narrative climax, social commentary track, opener/closer with thesis weight.

**Avoid/failure modes:** vague “deep” writing, sermon with no scene, no musical movement, overstuffed lyrics that cannot flow.

## artist.juice_wrld_emo_melodic_trap_lane

**Reference anchors:** Juice WRLD, emo rap, melodic trap, SoundCloud heartbreak rap, pop-punk rap.

**Core lane:** vulnerable melodic trap with freestyled-sounding sung-rap, guitar/piano melancholy, and highly repeatable hooks.

**Tempo:** 140–170 BPM counted fast; 70–85 half-time feel.

**Drums:** trap drums, crisp claps, active hats, soft but present 808 impact.

**Bass:** warm tuned 808 supporting the melody; simple slides allowed.

**Harmony/texture:** minor guitar loop, piano loop, emo/pop-punk chord color, occasionally bright melancholy.

**Vocal:** sung-rap lead, melodic phrases, spontaneous-feeling delivery, Auto-Tune/color acceptable, emotional cracks more valuable than polish.

**Lyric mode:** heartbreak, anxiety, late-night self-medication, obsessive love, confusion, vulnerability, pain as melody.

**Hook mechanics:** simple chorus with title phrase, open vowels, addictive melodic contour, repeated emotional contradiction.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A melodic emo-rap track in the Juice WRLD lane, with a minor guitar or piano loop, warm 808 sub, crisp trap drums, and vulnerable sung-rap vocals that feel spontaneous and catchy. The chorus should turn heartbreak and anxiety into a simple addictive hook with the title phrase repeated naturally.
```

**Album use:** lead single, emotional center, late-night confession, post-rupture track.

**Avoid/failure modes:** technical rap dominance, no sung hook, dense poetic verses, happy pop gloss, melodrama without specificity.

## artist.travis_scott_psychedelic_trap_lane

**Reference anchors:** Travis Scott, psychedelic trap, Houston/Atlanta arena trap, Auto-Tuned atmospheric rap.

**Core lane:** immersive psychedelic trap with Auto-Tuned vocals, reverb/delay, 808s, ad-libs, beat switches, and massive spatial production.

**Tempo:** 120–160 BPM; often half-time trap feel.

**Drums:** trap drums, punchy 808s, rolling hats, spacious percussion, occasional beat switch.

**Bass:** deep 808/sub, often huge but smooth.

**Harmony/texture:** spacey synths, dark pads, guitar/piano fragments, psychedelic atmosphere.

**Vocal:** Auto-Tuned melodic rap, ad-lib clouds, reverbed doubles, chantable phrases, layered energy.

**Lyric mode:** nightlife, excess, isolation, fame, surreal imagery, flex, escape.

**Arrangement:** intro atmosphere, hook/drop, verse pockets, beat switch or bridge optional, big re-entry.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A psychedelic trap track in a Travis Scott lane, with heavy Auto-Tuned melodic rap vocals, deep 808s, rolling trap hats, huge reverb, spacey synths, and ad-libs floating around the lead. The arrangement should feel immersive and cinematic, with a hook that opens into a massive atmospheric moment and an optional beat switch or breakdown.
```

**Avoid/failure modes:** dry vocal, small mix, no ad-libs, generic trap loop with no psychedelic space.

**Lyric mode:** toxic romance, ambition, substances, paranoia, street luxury, melancholy flex.

```text
A dark Atlanta melodic-trap track in a Future lane, with Auto-Tuned rap-singing used as the emotional center, heavy tuned 808s, rolling hi-hats, and a hypnotic minor synth loop. The hook should be repetitive, wounded, and anthemic, blurring flex, pain, and ambition.
```

## artist.drake_toronto_rap_rnb_lane

**Reference anchors:** Drake, OVO, Toronto rap/R&B, nocturnal melodic rap.

**Core lane:** rap storytelling blended with melodic choruses over moody, spacious R&B/hip-hop production.

**Tempo:** 65–100 BPM for moody cuts; 100–120 for dance/club hybrids.

**Drums:** sparse 808s, soft claps/snaps, atmospheric percussion; sometimes bounce/dancehall/pop variants.

**Bass:** warm sub, restrained.

**Harmony/texture:** cold pads, filtered samples, piano, low-pass atmosphere, nocturnal R&B chords.

**Vocal:** seamless rap-to-sung switches, conversational delivery, understated emotion, catchy but restrained hooks.

**Lyric mode:** introspection, relationship accounting, status anxiety, memory, city/nightlife, self-mythologizing.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A moody Toronto rap/R&B track in a Drake lane, with sparse 808 drums, cold atmospheric pads, warm sub bass, and a vocal that moves smoothly between conversational rap verses and a restrained melodic chorus. The lyric should feel introspective, nocturnal, and emotionally direct without losing swagger.
```

**Avoid/failure modes:** over-singing, too much percussion, no introspective detail, hook too theatrical.

**Lyric mode:** chaos, flex, vampiric/dark style, punk attitude, minimal repeatable phrases.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A rage-rap track in a Playboi Carti/Opium lane, with distorted 808s, bright serrated synth riffs, hard compressed trap drums, and a punk mosh-pit energy. The vocal should be phrase-based, repetitive, ad-lib-heavy, and chaotic, with attitude and sound taking priority over dense lyrics.
```

**Avoid/failure modes:** too lyrical, too slow, acoustic instruments, no distorted synth identity.
