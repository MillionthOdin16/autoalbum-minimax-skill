# Style Axis Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to make style fingerprints precise and non-interchangeable.

## Rhythm / groove axes
- straight vs swung,
- half-time vs double-time feel,
- four-on-the-floor vs trap grid vs drill bounce vs dembow vs shuffle,
- hat density: sparse / rolling / triplet / stuttered,
- snare/clap placement,
- syncopation level,
- pocket: behind / on top / rushed / lazy.

## Bass / 808 axes
- subby and smooth,
- clipped and punctuating,
- sliding/gliding,
- distorted and overdriven,
- melodic bassline,
- kick-layered punch,
- sustained drone.

## Harmony / melody axes
- diatonic major/minor,
- modal color,
- chromatic darkness,
- jazz/soul extensions,
- two-chord loop,
- sample-led harmony,
- synth ostinato,
- guitar/piano loop,
- ambient pad bed.

## Vocal axes
- dry spoken rap,
- conversational sing-rap,
- melodic Auto-Tune,
- shouted chant,
- whisper/intimate,
- nasal taunt,
- breathless punch-in,
- theatrical character switching,
- ad-lib forward,
- harmony stack.

## Hook axes
- chant/slogan,
- melodic chorus,
- post-chorus earworm,
- call-response,
- title phrase repetition,
- rhythmic catchphrase,
- drop-centered hook,
- atmospheric refrain.

## Arrangement axes
- loop-dominant,
- beat switches,
- gradual build,
- sudden stop/restart,
- drop/re-entry,
- bridge contrast,
- instrumental break,
- outro decay,
- sample flip.

## Mix / space axes
- dry and front-vocal,
- cavernous and atmospheric,
- distorted/overdriven,
- polished/glossy,
- lo-fi/grainy,
- wide stereo synths,
- narrow vocal club mix,
- sub-heavy car test.

## Lyric posture axes
- confessional,
- flex/luxury,
- threat/menace,
- moral inquiry,
- romantic toxicity,
- surreal persona,
- party command,
- diaristic pain,
- social observation,
- comedy/bravado.
