# Genre Templates: Pop, R&B, Soul, and Vocal-Forward Styles

## Global vocal-pop controls

- The hook must be singable after one listen.
- The title or title-adjacent phrase should usually appear in the chorus/hook.
- Use pre-chorus as tension ramp when the chorus needs lift.
- Use bridge only if it changes harmony, perspective, texture, or intensity.
- For MiniMax, keep vocal instructions in the prompt and lyrics cleanly sectioned.

---

## genre.contemporary_pop

**Tempo:** 90–125 BPM, or 120–128 for dance-pop.

**Groove:** clean, direct, rhythmically legible; designed for immediate recognition.

**Drums:** polished kick/snare/clap, tasteful percussion, modern punch.

**Bass:** strong simple bassline, often synth or electric, locked to hook.

**Harmony:** simple but emotionally effective progressions; major/minor contrast; pre-chorus lift.

**Vocal:** clear lead, layered chorus stacks, ad-libs in final chorus.

**Lyric posture:** direct, relatable, image + statement, title-driven chorus.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A polished contemporary pop song with a clear verse-pre-chorus-chorus lift, clean punchy drums, strong bass movement, and a lead vocal that becomes layered and memorable in the chorus. The hook should be immediate, title-centered, and easy to sing after one listen.
```

## genre.dark_pop_minimal

**Use when user says:** Billie Eilish, dark pop, whisper pop, minimalist pop, eerie intimate pop.

**Tempo:** 70–110 BPM; often slow or mid-tempo.

**Groove:** sparse, intimate, negative space; small sounds become hooks.

**Drums:** minimal kick/sub, snaps, foley, soft percussion, occasional distorted impact.

**Bass:** deep sub pulses, restrained but physical.

**Harmony:** minor, modal, chromatic or eerie; simple loops with tension.

**Vocal:** close-mic, breathy/whispered, understated; stacked harmonies only at payoff moments.

**Lyric posture:** macabre intimacy, secrecy, body/emotion detail, understated threat or vulnerability.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A minimalist dark-pop track in a Billie Eilish lane, with close whispered vocals, deep restrained sub bass, sparse foley-like percussion, and an eerie minor-key atmosphere. Keep the verses intimate and quiet, then let the chorus widen through layered harmonies without becoming generic dance-pop.
```

## genre.alt_rnb_atmospheric

**Use when user says:** SZA, Frank Ocean, alternative R&B, moody R&B, left-field R&B.

**Tempo:** 65–95 BPM, sometimes 100–120 for hybrid grooves.

**Groove:** pocketed, behind-the-beat, syncopated, spacious.

**Drums:** soft trap-influenced drums, tasteful percussion, subtle swing.

**Bass:** warm sub/electric bass, more cushion than punch.

**Harmony:** extended chords, 7ths/9ths/11ths, neo-soul motion, suspended color.

**Vocal:** intimate, conversational, imperfect, melismatic but not over-polished; background stacks and chopped/delayed vocal textures.

**Lyric posture:** diaristic, relational ambiguity, vulnerability, self-interrogation, nonlinear memory.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An atmospheric alternative R&B song with warm low end, soft syncopated drums, lush extended chords, and intimate conversational vocals that drift slightly around the pocket. The lyrics should feel diaristic and emotionally specific, with a hook that is vulnerable rather than over-polished.
```

## genre.dark_rnb_synthwave

**Use when user says:** The Weeknd, dark R&B, nocturnal synth-pop, Trilogy/Dawn FM lanes.

**Tempo:** 80–110 for dark R&B; 110–125 for synth-pop/dance lane.

**Groove:** brooding, nocturnal, sometimes four-on-floor; strong atmosphere.

**Drums:** electronic drums, punchy kick, gated/snappy snares, 80s/pop textures or sparse dark R&B.

**Bass:** rumbling sub, synth bass, sometimes driving octave pulse.

**Harmony:** minor, cinematic, synthwave, dream-pop/shoegaze color, dramatic chord movement.

**Vocal:** falsetto-capable, smooth, haunted, seductive, emotionally detached but intense.

**Lyric posture:** desire, alienation, nightlife, regret, fame/loneliness, self-destruction.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A dark R&B/synthwave track in a Weeknd-like lane, with haunted falsetto vocals, rumbling bass, nocturnal synths, and a cinematic late-night atmosphere. The beat should feel sleek and brooding, with a chorus that turns private desire and regret into a widescreen pop moment.
```

## genre.neo_soul_modern

**Tempo:** 70–100 BPM.

**Groove:** laid-back pocket, live-feeling drums, syncopated bass, strong musicianship.

**Drums:** organic kit or brushed/soft drums, tasteful percussion, swing.

**Bass:** electric bass, melodic and warm.

**Harmony:** rich extended chords, passing chords, chromatic color, gospel/jazz influence.

**Vocal:** soulful, dynamic, harmony stacks, ad-libs with intention.

**Lyric posture:** mature love, self-knowledge, spirituality, sensuality, vulnerability.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern neo-soul track with a deep pocket, warm electric bass, live-feeling drums, Rhodes-style extended chords, and expressive soulful vocals. The arrangement should breathe, leaving space for harmonies and tasteful ad-libs while the chorus lands with emotional maturity.
```

## genre.trap_soul

**Tempo:** 65–90 BPM.

**Groove:** slow, sparse, trap-influenced, sensual or moody.

**Drums:** 808s, snaps/claps, soft hats, minimal percussion.

**Bass:** warm 808/sub, often sustained.

**Harmony:** minor/ambiguous pads, electric piano, atmospheric samples.

**Vocal:** smooth R&B lead with rap-adjacent phrasing, airy stacks.

**Lyric posture:** late-night romance, regret, desire, vulnerability.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A slow trap-soul track with atmospheric pads, soft trap drums, warm 808 sub, and smooth intimate R&B vocals. The melody should feel late-night and emotionally direct, with a chorus that is sensual, simple, and repeatable.
```

## genre.hyperpop_digicore

**Tempo:** 130–170 BPM.

**Groove:** high-energy, glitchy, maximal, often trap/pop hybrid.

**Drums:** distorted drums, sharp claps, glitch fills, trap hats or club percussion.

**Bass:** distorted 808 or synthetic bass, sometimes sidechained.

**Harmony:** bright synth chords, pitch-shifted leads, major/minor emotional whiplash.

**Vocal:** pitched/formant-shifted, stacked, Auto-Tuned, compressed, expressive.

**Lyric posture:** internet-age emotion, chaos, heartbreak, identity, overstimulation.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A maximal hyperpop/digicore track with glossy distorted synths, hard digital drums, pitch-shifted vocal layers, and emotional pop songwriting pushed into chaotic electronic color. The hook should be huge, synthetic, and instantly replayable.
```
