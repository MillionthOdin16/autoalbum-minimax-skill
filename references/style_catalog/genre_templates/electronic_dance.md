# Genre Templates: Electronic and Dance

## Global dance controls

- Dance genres live or die by groove, build, drop, and texture.
- For MiniMax, specify whether the track is vocal, instrumental, or topline-led.
- Use `[Build Up]`, `[Break]`, `[Inst]`, `[Hook]`, and `[Outro]` intentionally.

---

## genre.house_classic_modern

**Tempo:** 120–130 BPM.

**Groove:** 4/4 four-on-the-floor kick, offbeat hats, clap/snare on 2 and 4, loop-based energy.

**Drums:** kick on every beat, claps, open hats, shakers, percussion.

**Bass:** repeating bassline, often synth/electric/funky.

**Harmony:** disco/soul/jazz/funk influenced chords; can be piano-house or synth-based.

**Vocal:** repeated phrases, diva hook, spoken vocal, or pop topline.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern house track at around 124 BPM with a steady four-on-the-floor kick, offbeat open hats, claps on the backbeat, a warm repeating bassline, and soulful chords. Use a simple vocal hook that repeats hypnotically and builds dancefloor momentum through layered percussion.
```

## genre.deep_house

**Tempo:** 118–124 BPM.

**Groove:** smooth four-on-floor, softer percussion, late-night pocket.

**Drums:** refined kick, hats, shakers, understated claps.

**Bass:** round sub/electric bass, groove-forward.

**Harmony:** lush minor/extended chords, pads, Rhodes, moody synths.

**Vocal:** restrained, airy, soulful, repeated phrases.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A deep house track with a smooth 122 BPM four-on-the-floor groove, warm sub bass, lush extended chords, airy pads, and restrained soulful vocals. Keep the energy hypnotic and elegant rather than festival-big.
```

---

## genre.tech_house

**Tempo:** 124–130 BPM.

**Groove:** driving house kick with techno-like minimalism and bass pressure.

**Drums:** punchy kick, tight hats, claps, minimal percs, risers.

**Bass:** repetitive, chunky, sidechained, hook-like bassline.

**Harmony:** minimal; focus on bass groove and vocal chops.

**Vocal:** short phrases, chopped hooks, crowd-ready repetition.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A tech-house club track around 126 BPM with a hard four-on-the-floor kick, tight percussion, a chunky repeating bassline, and minimal vocal chops. Build tension through filtered loops, snare rolls, and drops rather than complex chord changes.
```

---

## genre.techno_hypnotic

**Tempo:** 125–145 BPM.

**Groove:** repetitive, mechanical, hypnotic, kick-centered.

**Drums:** powerful mono kick, 909 hats/claps, industrial percussion, evolving automation.

**Bass:** rumbling low-end, rolling or pulsing, often one-note pressure.

**Harmony:** minimal, drones, stabs, atonal/industrial textures.

**Vocal:** usually minimal or absent; if present, spoken/fragmented.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A hypnotic techno track with a powerful four-on-the-floor kick, rolling low-end rumble, metallic percussion, and slowly evolving synth stabs. Keep the structure repetitive but constantly automating, with tension built through filters, noise, and rhythmic layering rather than pop songwriting.
```

## genre.melodic_techno

**Tempo:** 120–126 BPM.

**Groove:** steady techno/house pulse with emotional synth progression.

**Drums:** clean kick, driving hats, controlled percussion.

**Bass:** pulsing sidechained bass, low-end foundation.

**Harmony:** minor-key arpeggios, cinematic synth melodies, gradual evolution.

**Vocal:** minimal, atmospheric, or topline hook.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A melodic techno track around 123 BPM with a steady club pulse, pulsing sidechained bass, evolving minor-key arpeggios, and cinematic synth pads. Build slowly toward an emotional peak, using restrained vocals or vocal textures as atmosphere rather than a full pop lead.
```

## genre.dubstep_bass

**Tempo:** 138–142 BPM with half-time feel.

**Groove:** snare on beat 3, heavy build/drop, swung bass rhythms.

**Drums:** half-time kick/snare, aggressive fills, risers, impacts.

**Bass:** sub + wobble/modulated bass, growls, resampled textures.

**Harmony:** dark sparse chords, tension pads, cinematic build.

**Vocal:** chopped phrase, hook, or instrumental.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A heavy dubstep track at about 140 BPM with a half-time drum feel, snare on beat 3, huge sub bass, aggressive wobble bass movement, and a build-drop structure. Use dark cinematic tension in the intro and make the drop rhythmic, bass-focused, and physical.
```

## genre.drum_and_bass

**Tempo:** 160–180 BPM.

**Groove:** fast breakbeat, rolling drums, high kinetic energy.

**Drums:** chopped breaks, syncopated snares, ghost notes, fast hats.

**Bass:** Reese bass, sub pulses, neuro bass, or liquid bass.

**Harmony:** varies: soulful/liquid pads or dark neuro textures.

**Vocal:** optional; airy liquid topline or MC phrases.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A drum and bass track around 174 BPM with fast rolling breakbeats, deep sub bass, and high-speed momentum. Keep the drums intricate and propulsive, with either soulful atmospheric pads for liquid DnB or darker synthetic bass textures for a harder lane.
```

---

## genre.synthwave_retro

**Tempo:** 80–115 BPM or 110–125 for outrun.

**Groove:** 80s drum machine pulse, gated snares, arpeggiated synths.

**Drums:** Linn/808/909-style drums, gated reverb snare, tom fills.

**Bass:** octave synth bass, analog pulse.

**Harmony:** minor/nostalgic chords, neon atmosphere, cinematic progressions.

**Vocal:** optional; breathy pop, dark R&B, or instrumental.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A neon synthwave track with 80s drum-machine punch, gated snares, pulsing analog bass, and arpeggiated synths. The mood should feel cinematic, nocturnal, and nostalgic, with a wide chorus or instrumental hook that glows like city lights at night.
```
