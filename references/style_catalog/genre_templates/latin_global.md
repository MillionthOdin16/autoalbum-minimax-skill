# Genre Templates: Latin, Caribbean, African, and Global Pop

## Global note

For multilingual songs, put language intent in `project_config.json` and keep the MiniMax prompt in English unless the provider profile says otherwise. Lyrics can be Spanish, English, bilingual, Pidgin, Yoruba fragments, etc. if requested.

---

## genre.reggaeton_modern

**Tempo:** 85–100 BPM; some contemporary tracks faster.

**Groove:** dembow/tresillo bounce, steady and loop-forward.

**Drums:** dembow kick/snare/clap, layered percussion, ghost notes, fills.

**Bass:** clean tuned 808 or synth bass; supports the dembow.

**Harmony:** simple Latin-pop progressions, guitar/synth/piano hooks.

**Vocal:** melodic singing + rhythmic rap, confident, sensual, precise.

**Lyric posture:** desire, nightlife, dancing, swagger, relationship tension.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern reggaeton track around 92 BPM with a clean dembow groove, syncopated kick-snare pattern, tuned 808 bass, crisp percussion, and a melodic Spanish/English vocal that moves between singing and rhythmic rap. The hook should be sensual, confident, and danceable.
```

## genre.dominican_dembow

**Tempo:** 115–130 BPM.

**Groove:** faster, drums-forward dembow; loop-based and chant-heavy.

**Drums:** hard percussion, snares/claps, urgent dembow pulse.

**Bass:** heavy, sometimes distorted, minimal harmonic movement.

**Vocal:** rapid-fire chanting, call-response, high crowd energy.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A fast Dominican dembow track around 122 BPM with a drums-forward dembow rhythm, heavy bass, minimal loop-based melody, and rapid-fire chant vocals. Keep it percussive, repetitive, and built for crowd call-and-response.
```

## genre.latin_trap

**Tempo:** 120–160 BPM counted fast; 60–80 half-time.

**Groove:** trap drums with Latin melodic/vocal phrasing.

**Drums:** 808s, hi-hat rolls, claps, atmospheric percussion.

**Bass:** tuned 808s, slides, sub-heavy.

**Harmony:** dark minor guitar/piano/synth loops.

**Vocal:** Spanish melodic rap/sung-rap, Auto-Tune common, ad-libs.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A Latin trap track with dark minor guitar or piano loops, tuned 808 bass, crisp trap drums, and Spanish melodic rap vocals with Auto-Tuned emotional phrasing. The hook should be simple, hypnotic, and late-night.
```

## genre.afrobeats_modern

**Tempo:** 95–115 BPM.

**Groove:** syncopated percussion, relaxed dance pocket, cross-rhythms.

**Drums:** shakers, rim clicks, congas/toms, kick patterns that leave space.

**Bass:** warm, melodic bassline; often syncopated.

**Harmony:** guitar/piano/synth ostinatos, bright major/minor warmth.

**Vocal:** smooth melodic delivery, call-response, laid-back confidence.

**Lyric posture:** love, celebration, resilience, sensuality, lifestyle.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern Afrobeats track with a warm syncopated groove, dancing percussion, melodic bassline, clean guitar or piano ostinato, and smooth laid-back vocals. The hook should feel effortless, sunny, and rhythmically addictive rather than over-sung.
```

## genre.amapiano

**Tempo:** 105–115 BPM.

**Groove:** relaxed but danceable South African house pocket.

**Drums:** shakers, soft kick, percussive syncopation, log drum accents.

**Bass:** log drum acts as rhythmic bass/momentum element.

**Harmony:** jazzy piano chords, soulful pads, repetitive motifs.

**Vocal:** soulful, chant, call-response, or mostly instrumental.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An amapiano track around 112 BPM with a relaxed South African house groove, shuffling percussion, jazzy piano chords, soulful vocal phrases, and a deep log-drum bassline that creates the bounce. Let the arrangement unfold hypnotically through layers and call-response.
```

## genre.dancehall_modern

**Tempo:** 90–105 BPM.

**Groove:** syncopated riddim, offbeat accents, island bounce.

**Drums:** digital dancehall kit, percussion, rim/clap accents.

**Bass:** deep, repetitive, locked to riddim.

**Vocal:** melodic deejay/chat, patois if requested, rhythmic hooks.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern dancehall track with a syncopated riddim, deep repetitive bass, crisp digital percussion, and melodic deejay-style vocals. The hook should be rhythmic, playful, and built around a memorable chant.
```
