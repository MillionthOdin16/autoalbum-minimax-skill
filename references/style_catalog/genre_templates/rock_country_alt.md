# Genre Templates: Rock, Country, Indie, Folk, and Guitar-Based Styles

## Global guitar-band controls

- Specify guitar tone: clean, jangly, distorted, palm-muted, shoegaze, acoustic, twang, metal, etc.
- Specify drum feel: live rock backbeat, punk speed, country train beat, indie programmed hybrid.
- For MiniMax, avoid asking for too many guitar substyles at once.

---

## genre.pop_punk_emo

**Tempo:** 140–190 BPM.

**Groove:** fast 4/4, driving eighth-note guitars, explosive chorus.

**Drums:** live kit, punchy snare, crash cymbals, fills into chorus.

**Bass:** eighth-note root movement, locked with guitars.

**Harmony:** power chords, I-V-vi-IV variants, minor/major angst shifts.

**Vocal:** urgent, nasal/bright, shouted-sung chorus, gang vocals optional.

**Lyric posture:** heartbreak, resentment, youth, self-doubt, confession, cathartic one-liners.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A high-energy pop-punk/emo anthem with fast live drums, driving distorted guitars, punchy bass, and an urgent shouted-sung lead vocal. The chorus should explode into a cathartic hook with simple direct lyrics and a title phrase built for crowd singalong.
```

## genre.indie_rock_modern

**Tempo:** 90–140 BPM.

**Groove:** live or hybrid drums, guitar-forward, human imperfections.

**Drums:** live kit, restrained fills, roomy snare.

**Bass:** melodic but understated.

**Harmony:** guitar chords, occasional synth/organ, modal color.

**Vocal:** conversational, imperfect, expressive, sometimes understated.

**Lyric posture:** personal detail, irony, memory, relationship fragments, existential images.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern indie rock track with live-feeling drums, jangly and slightly overdriven guitars, melodic bass, and a conversational lead vocal. Keep the performance human and textured, with a chorus that grows naturally rather than becoming polished arena pop.
```

---

## genre.alt_rock_grunge

**Tempo:** 80–150 BPM.

**Groove:** heavy backbeat, quiet-loud dynamics, distorted guitars.

**Drums:** live kit, strong snare on 2 and 4, crash-heavy choruses.

**Bass:** gritty, supportive, sometimes melodic.

**Harmony:** power chords, minor/mode shifts, dissonance.

**Vocal:** raw, strained, emotional, less polished.

**Lyric posture:** alienation, frustration, irony, confession, social unease.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An alternative rock/grunge track with quiet-loud dynamics, gritty distorted guitars, live drums with a heavy backbeat, and raw emotionally strained vocals. Let the verses feel tense and restrained, then make the chorus explode with cathartic distortion.
```

---

## genre.shoegaze_dream_pop

**Tempo:** 80–130 BPM.

**Groove:** dreamy, washed, less rhythm-forward unless hybrid.

**Drums:** roomy live or programmed drums, often softened.

**Bass:** warm and anchoring.

**Harmony:** lush chords, suspended/dreamy voicings, wall of sound.

**Guitars:** reverb, delay, chorus, fuzz, tremolo, layered texture.

**Vocal:** buried/ethereal, soft, melodic, part of texture.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A shoegaze/dream-pop track with layered reverb-drenched guitars, soft buried vocals, warm bass, and a hazy wall-of-sound atmosphere. Keep the melody emotionally clear underneath the wash, with a chorus that blooms rather than punches.
```

## genre.country_traditional

**Tempo:** 70–115 BPM.

**Groove:** two-step, train beat, waltz, or mid-tempo backbeat.

**Drums:** acoustic kit, brushed/snare train beat, natural dynamics.

**Bass:** upright or electric, simple supportive movement.

**Harmony:** I-IV-V, vi, secondary dominants, simple clear changes.

**Instruments:** acoustic guitar, fiddle, pedal steel, banjo, dobro, mandolin.

**Vocal:** clear storytelling, twang if requested, intimate or conversational.

**Lyric posture:** story, place, family, love, loss, work, regret, plainspoken detail.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A traditional country song with acoustic guitar, pedal steel, fiddle accents, warm bass, and a natural live drum feel. The vocal should be plainspoken and story-driven, with a memorable hook built from concrete details about love, loss, place, or hard-earned regret.
```

---

## genre.country_pop_modern

**Tempo:** 75–120 BPM.

**Groove:** polished country rhythm with pop hook structure; sometimes trap-influenced drums.

**Drums:** live kit blended with programmed claps/snaps/808 textures if desired.

**Bass:** electric bass, sub support in modern lane.

**Harmony:** country-pop progressions, guitar hooks, big chorus lift.

**Instruments:** acoustic guitar, electric guitar, banjo/fiddle/pedal steel accents, pop synth pads.

**Vocal:** clear, emotional, radio-friendly, twang optional.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
A modern country-pop song with acoustic guitar, polished live drums, subtle banjo or pedal steel color, and a big radio-ready chorus. The lyric should tell a concrete personal story with a title-centered hook, while the production balances country instrumentation with modern pop punch.
```

---

## genre.folk_acoustic_singer_songwriter

**Tempo:** 60–110 BPM.

**Groove:** acoustic strum or fingerpicking, organic timing.

**Drums:** none, brushed, or soft percussion.

**Bass:** subtle upright/electric or absent.

**Harmony:** simple, emotionally clear chords; occasional modal color.

**Vocal:** intimate, honest, close-mic, little processing.

**Lyric posture:** narrative, confession, nature/place, memory, moral realization.

**MiniMax prompt fragment:**

```text
An intimate acoustic singer-songwriter track with fingerpicked guitar, minimal percussion, warm close vocals, and plainspoken lyrics full of concrete images. Let the song build through emotional detail and harmonic subtlety rather than big production.
```
